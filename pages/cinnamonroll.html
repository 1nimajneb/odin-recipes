Cinnamon Roll

Ingredients

This is a simple recipe that only uses four ingredients!

  • Tortillas
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar

Instructions

Simple instructions for simple people

  1. Spread butter on a tortilla.
  2. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar over butter
  3. Microwave until butter melts (about 15 seconds).
  4. Roll up and enjoy.

It's got cinnamon and you roll it up.
