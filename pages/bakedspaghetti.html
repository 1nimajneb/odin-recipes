Simple Baked Spaghetti & Cheese

Ingredients

This is a simple recipe that only uses two ingredients!

  • Spaghetti
  • Shredded Cheese

Instructions

Simple instructions for simple people

  1. Cook & drain spaghetti following instructions on the package.
  2. Layer spaghetti and shredded cheese in a 9 x 13 baking dish.
  3. Bake for 40 minutes at 350 degrees F.
  4. If you want to get fancy you can season with salt and pepper.
