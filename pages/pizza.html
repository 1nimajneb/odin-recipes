Simple Pizza

Ingredients

This is a simple recipe that only uses three ingredients!

  • Tortillas
  • Tomato Paste
  • Shredded Cheese

Instructions

Simple instructions for simple people

  1. Spread tomato paste on tortilla.
  2. Cover tomato paste in shredded cheese.
  3. Toast in oven until cheese is melty.
